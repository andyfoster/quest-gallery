Quest 2018

Held at the Son Tinh Camp 40km from Ha Noi, Quest Festival is an outdoor celebration of music, art, and culture. The creative theme for 2018 drew inspiration from the genesis story of Thần Trụ Trời, a legend of Vietnamese folklore, with pillars of 'sky above' and 'earth below'.

Unfortunately, this festival was cancelled at the last minute but this is a tribute to the work that everyone did to make it the most amazing festival that never happened.
